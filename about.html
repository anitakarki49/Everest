The History of Shilajit

While pure shilajit is easily accessible today, it held immense value in ancient times and could only be obtained with gold. Its origins lie in the Himalayan and Altai mountain ranges, and its usage has been recorded for over 5000 years. Historical texts even attributed its creation to the Hindu god Shiva, associating shilajit with divine power. The mere sight of shilajit intrigued people, as it naturally seeped from rock crevices, earning intriguing names like "mountain blood" and "rock sweat." As its versatile applications became more apparent, the demand for authentic shilajit grew, making it a popular component in many individuals' daily health and wellness routines.
